Bear

Wild brown bears use rocks as a tool to scratch an itchy spot.

  • Scientific name: Ursidae
  • Average height: 70 to 153 cm
  • Mass: 100-150kg
  • Lifespan: 25 years

Large bodies with stocky legs, long snouts, small rounded ears, shaggy hair, plantigrade paws with five nonretractile claws, and short tails.
